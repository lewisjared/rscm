Added multi-model ocean carbon IRF support with `IrfForm` enum (Polynomial, ExponentialSum) and `OceanCarbonModel` enum (3D-GFDL, 2D-BERN, HILDA). Switched the default ocean carbon model from 2D-BERN to 3D-GFDL to match MAGICC7's default configuration, and fixed the temperature sensitivity parameter from 0.0423 to 0.03717879 (`OCEANCC_TEMPFEEDBACK`).
