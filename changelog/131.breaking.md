Restructured terrestrial carbon cycle from 4 pools (plant, detritus, soil, humus) to 3 pools (plant, detritus, soil) to match MAGICC7. The `humus_pool_pi`, `humus_temp_sensitivity`, and `frac_soil_to_humus` parameters have been removed. The `enable_fertilization` and `enable_temp_feedback` boolean parameters have been replaced by `fertilization_yrstart` and `tempfeedback_yrstart` year-based controls.
